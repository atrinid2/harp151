UPDATE:

So, I've offically had my ESA for a whole week. I've learned a lot more about her.

She wants to eat absolutely everything. Like she will dig out wrappers just to lick them clean. I've had to buy lids for all my trash cans.

When she gets more comfortable, she likes to be vocal. She didn't meow at all at first, even when we drove her up to Binghamton, but now she meows and chirps for anything.

She's a cuddly cat. She loves sleeping on my bed with me, which makes me so so happy. She slept on my back the other day.

Overall, she's been great so far! Can't wait to spend the rest of the semester with her!
